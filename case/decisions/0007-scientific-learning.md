# Run the plot as field studies

## Context
How we learn from the work. The model frames a learning loop (see [learning](../../template/learning/00-overview.md)).

## Decision
Treat every significant prediction about the land as a hypothesis: set a fair test and success criteria in advance, measure honestly, and feed the result back into the model, confirmed or refuted.

## Why
It keeps us honest, turns the plot into a set of real studies rather than impressions, and makes our results worth something to others.

## Constraints
Small scale and two observers; studies stay simple and their limits stated.

## Revisit when
The loop becomes busywork that never changes a decision.
