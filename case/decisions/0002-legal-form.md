# Legal form

## Context

We need an entity to own land and take in grants and donations, but the right form depends on the country, and that is still open ([candidate countries](0001-candidate-countries.md)).

## Options considered

The forms in play follow our two candidates (see [legal entity](../../template/organization/legal-entity.md)): an e.V. in Germany, an Associação in Portugal, with a charitable company or IPSS as later possibilities if we earn income or qualify.

## Decision

Defer the choice. Stay informal and lean until we either commit to a country or hit a moment that forces an entity, such as a grant award or a land deal. When that comes, default to the simplest fitting non-profit in the chosen country.

## Why

Form follows country, and committing now would just lock in guesses. The cost of waiting is low while we are pre-funding and pre-site.

## Constraints

- Pre-funding and pre-site; no pressing need for an entity yet.
- Whatever we pick must be able to hold land and receive grants.

## Revisit when

- We commit to a country.
- A grant or land opportunity requires a registered entity.
