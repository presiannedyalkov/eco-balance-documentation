# Ecosystem first, modest income base case

## Context
What the project runs on financially. The model weighs revenue options by realism (see [economics/revenue](../../template/economics/revenue.md)).

## Decision (proposed)
Base case: founder capacity plus modest near-term income — research access, education, small grants, donations. Treat carbon credits, energy sales, and tourism as upside, never the plan.

## Why
Speculative income has sunk small projects before, and the ecosystem, not a revenue stream, is the point. Carbon markets in particular are slow and easy to oversell at our scale.

## Constraints
No large capital, two part-time people, a long horizon.

## Revisit when
A concrete scheme — a real payments-for-ecosystem-services programme, a funded partnership — makes an income line dependable.
