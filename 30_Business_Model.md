## Cost Structure

### Operating Principles
- **Keep overhead low:** <15% administrative costs
- **Maximize impact spending:** >85% program costs
- **Build reserves:** Target 6 months by Year 3
- **Invest in capacity:** Staff, systems, infrastructure

### Major Expense Categories

**Personnel (60-70%):**
- Restoration staff
- Management team
- Education coordinators

**Operations (15-20%):**
- Land maintenance
- Equipment and supplies
- Utilities and insurance

**Capital (10-15%):**
- Infrastructure expansion
- Equipment purchases
- Technology upgrades
