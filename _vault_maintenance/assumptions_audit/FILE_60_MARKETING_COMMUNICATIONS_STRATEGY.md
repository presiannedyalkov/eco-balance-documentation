## Executive Summary

This file outlines a comprehensive marketing and communications strategy with **CRITICAL capacity assumptions** for a 1-2 person founding team. The document assumes effectiveness of multiple marketing channels without validation, assumes audience receptivity without market research, and presents an ambitious 90-day launch plan that may be unrealistic for a bootstrap operation. Key questions: Can a tiny team execute this strategy? Will chosen channels actually work for this specific project? Is there budget for marketing execution?

## Critical Assumptions (Priority: CRITICAL)

### 1. Team Capacity to Execute Comprehensive Strategy

**Quote**: "Weekly: 2-3 social media posts, 1 story or update... Monthly: Newsletter to subscribers, Blog post on website, Video update... Quarterly: Comprehensive progress report..."
**Location**: Section "Content Calendar"
**Type**: Team/Resource/Timeline

**Assumption**: A 1-2 person founding team (per strategic/01_Executive_Summary.md) can execute a comprehensive multi-channel marketing strategy while simultaneously running restoration operations, fundraising, site selection, legal setup, and all other startup activities.

**Alternatives**:
- Current: Full marketing strategy from Day 1 with weekly/monthly content across all channels
- Option B: Minimal viable marketing (one primary channel only) until team expands in Year 2
- Option C: Hire marketing consultant/contractor specifically for Year 1 (add €5-10k to budget)

**Critical Question**: How will 1-2 founders simultaneously manage restoration operations AND produce 2-3 social posts/week, monthly newsletters, video updates, blog posts, donor communications, and quarterly reports?

**Impact if Wrong**: Marketing strategy becomes a paper plan that's never executed, or operations suffer because founders spend time on marketing instead of core restoration work.
**Uncertainty**: HIGH

**Location**: Section "90-Day Marketing Launch Plan"

## Agent Notes

This file presents one of the most ambitious assumption sets in the entire documentation: a comprehensive, professional marketing strategy for a bootstrap project with 1-2 founders. The disconnect between the sophisticated strategy outlined and the resource constraints of a €20-30k, 1-2 person operation is significant.

**Key insight**: This appears to be a "complete/ideal" marketing strategy rather than a "Year 1 bootstrap" marketing strategy. Consider creating a separate "Bootstrap Marketing Plan" focused on the 1-3 most effective channels only, with minimal time investment.

**Recommendation for User**: Choose ONE primary marketing channel for Year 1 (likely LinkedIn OR local Portuguese media), execute it well, and expand only after operations are stable and funding allows.
