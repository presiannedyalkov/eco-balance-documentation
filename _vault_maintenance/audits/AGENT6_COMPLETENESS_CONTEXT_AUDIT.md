- Timeline: Four pathway options (Fast/Gradual/Slow/Opportunity)
- Funding: Six pathways (Crowdfunding/Grant/Angel/Prize/Bootstrap/Partnership)

---

### 2. Bootstrap Model Clarity ✅
**Observation:** The "bootstrap model" concept is consistently explained across files.

**Core Elements Consistently Defined:**
- 1 hectare pilot site (not 10-30 hectares)
- 1-2 founders doing work (not 5-10 person team)
- €20-30k Year 1 budget (not €100-500k)
- Proof-of-concept focus (not immediate scaling)
- 100-200 trees Year 1 partial year (realistic expectations)

**Assessment:** Bootstrap philosophy is well-articulated and prevents misunderstanding of project scale.

---

### 3. Technical Term Definition ✅
**Observation:** Technical terms are generally well-defined on first use.

**Examples of Good Practice:**
- "Assisted Natural Regeneration (ANR)" - defined as protecting existing seedlings
- "MPPT controller" - explained as Maximum Power Point Tracking for solar efficiency
- "IBC tanks" - specified as 1,000L intermediate bulk containers
- "LiFePO4 batteries" - described as lithium iron phosphate, safer chemistry
- "gGmbH" - explained as gemeinnützige GmbH, non-profit limited liability company

**Assessment:** Strong technical communication with appropriate context for non-expert readers.

---

### 4. Budget Transparency ✅
**Observation:** All files with budget information provide clear ranges and justify variances.

**Examples:**
- Technology: "€10-18k depending on solar option chosen (A/B/C)"
- Restoration: "€2.2-3.5k, higher in Mediterranean due to water infrastructure"
- Year 1 Total: "€20-30k standard bootstrap, €10-15k ultra-lean possible"

**Assessment:** Budget ranges are transparent, explained, and traceable to specific choices.

---

### 5. Cross-Reference Pattern ✅
**Observation:** Files consistently use helpful cross-reference pattern.

**Good Pattern (prevalent):**
- "→ See: [[strategic/21_Technology_Integration|Water Systems Detail]]" - tells reader what additional detail is available
- "→ Details: [[strategic/32_Funding_Strategy|Crowdfunding Execution Plan]]" - indicates what the linked file contains

**Less Helpful Pattern (rare):**
- "As discussed in X" - requires reader to find and read other document
- "See X above" - assumes linear reading

**Assessment:** Cross-references are generally well-handled with contextual descriptions.
