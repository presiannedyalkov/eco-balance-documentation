## üéØ Decision Framework

### When Things Go Wrong

**Minor Setback (10-20% below target):**
- **Example:** Grant denied, plant survival 75% not 85%
- **Response:** Adjust, learn, continue
- **No major changes needed**

**Moderate Challenge (20-40% below target):**
- **Example:** Half expected funding secured, multiple grants fail
- **Response:** Reassess timeline, reduce scope, intensify efforts
- **Some adaptation needed**

**Major Crisis (>40% below target):**
- **Example:** Can't secure land, funding collapses, team dissolves
- **Response:** Pause, reassess fundamentally, consider pivot or closure
- **Major restructuring required**

**Key Decision Points:**
- Month 3: Legal and funding progress check
- Month 6: Go/no-go on land acquisition
- Month 9: Team and operations check
- Month 12: Overall Year 1 assessment
