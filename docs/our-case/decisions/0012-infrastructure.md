# Living systems wait for the parcel

## Context
Where and how we live on the land — dwelling, power, water, connection. The model gives the options at each tier (see [infrastructure](../../model/infrastructure/00-overview.md)).

## Decision (deferred)
No specific choices yet. Dwelling type, power setup, and water systems all depend on the actual parcel — what it has, what it allows, and its water story. The model's options stand as the menu.

## Why
These are site-shaped decisions; making them before there is a site would only be undone.

## Constraints
Budget puts the dwelling as the second-largest cost after land; everything else stays minimal.

## Revisit when
A site is secured and its buildings, water, and grid access are known.
