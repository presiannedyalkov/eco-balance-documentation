# Eco Balance

Restoring worn-out land into living habitat, and sharing how, so others can do the same.

We are two people bringing a few hectares of degraded land back to life, living on it, and handing it on in better shape than we found it. The work runs in two parts, kept deliberately separate.

A reusable guide to this kind of restoration: the options, their trade-offs, and the evidence behind them. Fork it and start your own. → [Start here](model/foundation/why-restore-degraded-land.md) · [Try the explorer](/explorer)

What we are actually doing — the choices we have made and why, and where things stand right now. → [Our vision](our-case/vision.md) · [Status](our-case/status.md)
