## Project Vision

Eco Balance is an ambitious holistic environmental restoration initiative designed to combat climate change through ecosystem regeneration. Unlike conventional reforestation projects, Eco Balance takes an integrated systems approach that addresses soil health, water management, biodiversity conservation, community engagement, and technological innovation simultaneously.

---

## Core Mission

To restore degraded ecosystems in Southern Europe through evidence-based, community-engaged, financially sustainable environmental restoration that serves as a replicable model for global climate action.

## Strategic Objectives (Years 1-10)

### Year 1: Prove Concept (Bootstrap Pilot)
- Establish legal entity (gGmbH)
- Secure first land (1 hectare, with expansion rights to 5-10 hectares)
- Build minimal viable pilot site

### Years 2-3: Demonstrate Impact
- Measurable ecological improvements
- Financial sustainability model proven

### Years 4-5: Scale Operations
- Expand to 100+ hectares
- Multiple sites operational

### Years 6-10: Global Expansion
- Policy influence achieved
- 10,000+ hectares managed
- Model replicated globally

- **Climate Change:** Temperatures rising, extreme weather increasing
- **Biodiversity Loss:** 73% decline in wildlife populations since 1970
- **Soil Degradation:** 33% of global soils degraded
- **Water Scarcity:** 2 billion people lack safe drinking water
