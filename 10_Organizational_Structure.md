## üîß Registration Process

### Step-by-Step

**1. Choose Name**
- Must indicate charitable purpose
- Check availability (BMJV website)
- Not same as existing organization
- Example: "Eco Balance gGmbH"

**2. Draft Articles of Association**
Must include:
- Name and registered office
- Charitable purpose statement
- Share capital structure
- Shareholder details
- Director appointments
- Financial management rules
- Dissolution provisions

**3. Notarization**
- German notary must officially record articles
- All shareholders must be present or represented
- Notary verifies identity and intent
- Cost: ~‚Ç¨500-‚Ç¨1,000

**4. Commercial Register**
- Submit notarized documents
- Register with local Handelsregister
- Processing time: 4-8 weeks
- Registration fee: ~‚Ç¨150-‚Ç¨300

**5. Tax Registration**
- Register with local tax office (Finanzamt)
- Submit tax registration questionnaire
- Provide articles of association
- Extract from commercial register
- Opening balance sheet

**6. Obtain Non-Profit Status**
- Apply for provisional non-profit status
- Tax office reviews charitable purpose
- Final status granted retroactively
- Processing: 3-6 months

**7. Additional Registrations**
- EU VAT number (if applicable)
- Register with BZSt (Federal Central Tax Office)
- Professional liability insurance
- Bank account setup

**Total Timeline:** 3-6 months  
**Total Cost:** ‚Ç¨15,000-‚Ç¨20,000 (including legal counsel)
