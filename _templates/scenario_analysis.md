## Scenario Overview

This analysis models three scenarios representing different probability bands:

- **Best Case** (~5-15% probability): Everything works as hoped
- **Realistic Case** (~60-80% probability): Typical obstacles encountered
- **Worst Case** (~15-35% probability): Major components fail

For each scenario, we detail assumptions, timeline, outcomes, and required actions.
