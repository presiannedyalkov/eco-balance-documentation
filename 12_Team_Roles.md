## üîÑ Hiring Process

### Recruitment Timeline

**Phase 1 (Year 1)**: No external hiring
- Founders execute all roles
- Volunteer support where available
- Build network and identify potential future hires
- Document all processes for future team onboarding

**Phase 2 (Year 2)**: First professional hires
- **Month 12-15:** Restoration Specialist recruitment (often first hire)
  - Define job description
  - Post position
  - Interview and hire
- **Month 15-18:** Project Manager (if not founder)
  - Begin recruitment process
  - Onboarding and integration

**Phase 3 (Year 3)**: Full professional team
- **Year 3 Q1-Q2:** Operations/Community role
- **Year 3 Q3-Q4:** Financial/Admin role (as budget allows)

---

### Where to Post

**General:**
- LinkedIn
- Indeed.de
- Stepstone.de

**Environmental Sector:**
- Conservation Job Board
- Environmental Careers
- Greenjobs.de
- ReliefWeb (international NGO)

**Academic:**
- University job boards
- Research institution listings
- Society for Ecological Restoration

**Networks:**
- Advisory Board connections
- Partner organization referrals
- Professional associations

---

### Selection Process

**Stage 1: Application Review**
- Resume and cover letter
- Shortlist top 10 candidates

**Stage 2: Phone Screening**
- 30-minute initial conversation
- Basic fit assessment
- Narrow to 5 candidates

**Stage 3: In-Person Interview**
- 90-minute structured interview
- Case study or presentation
- Site visit
- Narrow to 2-3 finalists

**Stage 4: Final Selection**
- Reference checks (3 references)
- Board interview (for ED)
- Background check
- Offer decision

**Timeline:** 3-4 months per position

## üìà Organizational Growth

### Scaling Principles

**Quality Over Speed:**
- Hire slowly, fire fast
- Never compromise on fit
- Build strong culture early
- Invest in onboarding

**Capacity Building:**
- Clear roles and responsibilities
- Systems and processes documented
- Cross-training for resilience
- Succession planning

**Sustainability:**
- Revenue growth supports hiring
- 70-80% of budget on staff sustainable
- Build reserves before expansion
- Adjust if needed
