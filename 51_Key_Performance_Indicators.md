## KPI Philosophy

**What gets measured gets managed.** KPIs ensure accountability, track progress, and enable data-driven decisions.

### Our Approach:
- **Measurable:** Quantifiable metrics
- **Meaningful:** Aligned with mission
- **Actionable:** Drive improvements
- **Transparent:** Publicly reported

- Survival rate <70% ‚Üí Review species selection and care
