## PROJECT AT A GLANCE

### Vision
Holistic environmental restoration to combat climate change through ecosystem regeneration in Southern Europe, creating a replicable model for global restoration.

### Mission
Restore degraded ecosystems through science-based, community-engaged restoration that:
- Sequesters carbon and protects biodiversity
- Creates sustainable livelihoods
- Integrates renewable technology
- Achieves financial sustainability
- Scales globally

### Unique Approach
Unlike typical tree-planting projects, Eco Balance:
- Rebuilds entire ecosystems (soil, water, biodiversity, community)
- Integrates technology (solar, atmospheric water, IoT monitoring)
- Prioritizes financial sustainability (eco-tourism, carbon credits, energy sales)
- Designed for replication and scaling
- Emphasizes rigorous science and monitoring

## KEY RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### TOP 5 RISKS

**1. Insufficient Funding**
- **Mitigation:** Diversify sources, start small if needed, consider lease vs. purchase

**2. Land Acquisition Challenges**
- **Mitigation:** Evaluate 10+ sites, have backups, consider partnership models

**3. Ecological Setbacks (drought, fire, pests)**
- **Mitigation:** Native species, water systems, monitoring, adaptive management

**4. Slow Revenue Development**
- **Mitigation:** Conservative projections, multiple streams, maintain grants initially

**5. Community Resistance**
- **Mitigation:** Early engagement, local employment, transparent communication
