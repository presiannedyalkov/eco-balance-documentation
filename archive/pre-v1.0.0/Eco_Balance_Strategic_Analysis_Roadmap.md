## 4. FUNDING STRATEGY DEEP DIVE

### 4.1 Realistic Fundraising Assessment

**The Hard Truth:** Environmental restoration projects typically take 12-24 months to secure initial operating capital. Here's why:

**Grant Challenges:**
- Most grants require registered legal entity
- Application processes take 3-6 months (or longer)
- Success rates often 10-30%
- Competition is fierce
- Reporting requirements are intensive

**Corporate Partnership Challenges:**
- CSR budgets set annually (long lead times)
- Require established track record
- Prefer working with known entities
- Often support operations, not startup capital

**Individual Donor Challenges:**
- Building donor base takes time
- Major gifts require relationship building
- High-net-worth individuals cautious with new organizations
- Annual giving programs need cultivation

**Crowdfunding Challenges:**
- Success requires existing community/platform
- €50,000+ campaigns rare without PR budget
- Requires intensive campaign effort
- Platform fees reduce net (5-10%)

**The Good News:** Your project has strong fundamentals for fundraising:
- Compelling vision
- Scientific credibility
- Multiple revenue streams planned
- Holistic approach differentiated
- Clear impact metrics
