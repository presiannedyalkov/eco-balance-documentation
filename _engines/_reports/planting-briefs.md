# Planting briefs

A starting palette per bioregion, generated from the parameter profiles. Refine to the actual site once chosen.

## Germany

- **Climate zone:** temperate
- **Target ecosystem:** oak–beech–hornbeam mixed temperate broadleaf forest
- **Planting window:** Oct–Nov (dormant) and Mar–Apr
- **Plausible hectares:** 1–2
- **Key hazards:** summer_drought, bark_beetle, storms
- **Starting palette (9):** Quercus robur, Quercus petraea, Fagus sylvatica, Carpinus betulus, Tilia cordata, Betula pendula, Corylus avellana, Sorbus aucuparia, Prunus avium

## Portugal

- **Climate zone:** mediterranean
- **Target ecosystem:** cork oak / holm oak / pine montado and dryland forest
- **Planting window:** Oct–Feb (after summer drought, before spring)
- **Plausible hectares:** 3–5
- **Key hazards:** summer_drought, wildfire, water_scarcity, rural_depopulation
- **Starting palette (8):** Quercus suber, Quercus rotundifolia, Quercus faginea, Pinus pinea, Arbutus unedo, Cistus, Rosmarinus, Myrtus communis
