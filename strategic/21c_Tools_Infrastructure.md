### Decision Framework: Which Technology Scenario?

**Determined by**:
1. **Total budget after land purchase?** ‚Üí Dictates scenario (A/B/C)
2. **Location climate?** ‚Üí Temperate vs Mediterranean water strategy
3. **Site conditions?** ‚Üí Grid access? Well possible? Municipal water?
4. **Timeline commitment?** ‚Üí 1-2 years (lean toward A), 5+ years (justify C)

**Current Status**:
- **Planning for Scenario B** (‚Ç¨15-20k, standard bootstrap) as baseline
- **Flexible to Scenario A** if crowdfunding falls short
- **Flexible to Scenario C** if major grant or crowdfunding exceeds expectations
- **Final technology decisions**: Deferred until total budget and site confirmed (Month 6-9)
