# Team

For a long time the team is the founders, part-time. The real question is how to cover the skills you need without spending money you don't have, and how to grow only when the work and income justify it.

## Non-negotiables

- Don't scale paid staff ahead of revenue.
- Cover the essential skills somehow.
- Guard against burnout — two people doing everything is the real constraint.

## Options & pathways

- **Part-time founders keeping jobs** — the usual start; safe and patient, limited by spare hours.
- **Reduced hours / transition** — once some project income exists; more capacity, more exposure.
- **Full-time** — only when stable income can pay a living wage.
- **Hybrid or seasonal** — match effort to planting peaks.

## Filling gaps without hiring

Learning (cheap, compounding), a small advisory board (free expertise), contractors for what must be right (an accountant above all), volunteers for events and monitoring, and partnerships for skills and credibility. Add paid people only against income that has been stable for a sustained stretch, not one good month — the thresholds belong in [economics](../economics/00-overview.md).
